About the AI Health Mirror

Our mission is to leverage the power of artificial intelligence to promote daily wellness and mindfulness. In a world where screen time is ever-increasing, it's easy to lose touch with our own emotional and physical state. The AI Health Mirror serves as a gentle, real-time companion to help you stay aware.

Our Technology

This application is built with a Python and Flask backend, running sophisticated computer vision models with TensorFlow and MediaPipe. The frontend is a responsive web interface created with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, communicating with the server in real-time via WebSockets.
